dipbackground

Calculate dipolar EPR (DEER) background signal.

Syntax
Vinter = dipbackground(t,conc,lambda)
dipbackground(...)
Description
W

dipbackground calculates the inter-molecular DEER signal for a homogeneous three-dimensional distribution of spins.

t is the time vector, in microseconds. conc is the spin concentration, in micromolar (µM). lambda is the modulation depth, between 0 and 1.

The output Vinter is the inter-molecular dipolar background decay function, scaled such that it is equal to 1 at time zero.

Examples

Here is a typical application, for a 50 µM spin concentration and a modulation depth of 0.3: 

t = -0.2:0.020:5;  % µs
Vinter = dipbackground(t,100,0.3);
plot(t,Vinter)
