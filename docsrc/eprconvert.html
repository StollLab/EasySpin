eprconvert

Conversion between frequency, field and g values.

Syntax
eprconvert
Description

This graphical interface can convert frequency and fields to other units. Frequency units are MHz, GHz, cm-1 and eV. Field units are mT, T, G and kG.

In addition, any of the three quantities field, frequency and g value can be computed from the other two.
